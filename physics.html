poolvr.physics: event-based physics engine¶

Event-based pool physics simulation engine based on the paper (available at http://web.stanford.edu/group/billiards/AnEventBasedPoolPhysicsSimulator.pdf):

AN EVENT-BASED POOL PHYSICS SIMULATOR Will Leckie, Michael Greenspan DOI: 10.1007/11922155_19 · Source: DBLP Conference: Advances in Computer Games, 11th International Conference, Taipei, Taiwan, September 6-9, 2005.
class poolvr.physics.PoolPhysics(num_balls=16, ball_mass=0.17, ball_radius=0.028575, mu_r=0.016, mu_sp=0.044, mu_s=0.2, e=0.93, g=9.81, initial_positions=None, **kwargs)[source]¶

Pool physics simulator

Parameters:
  • mu_r – friction coefficient (rolling)
  • mu_sp – friction coefficient (spining)
  • mu_s – friction coefficient (sliding)
  • e – coefficient of restitution for ball collisions
  • g – gravity
